ABOUT US

PRIMA PLATINUM SDN BHD, established in 2006, is a leading security agency in Malaysia providing comprehensive security solutions with nationwide branches. All security personnel undergo a strict recruitment process, are vetted by the Royal Malaysian Police, and receive intensive training before deployment. With extensive industry experience and quality service delivery, we serve clients across industrial, manufacturing, telecommunication sectors, as well as offices and corporate firms.

Mission

To providing high-quality and reliable security services through continuous innovation, a commitment to positive change, and a focus on long-term sustainability and responsible practices.



Vision

To become a key player in the property and facilities management industry by delivering quality, smart living environments that empower and enhance the well-being of communities.
